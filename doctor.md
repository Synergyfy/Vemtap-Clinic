
  1. The "Availability Settings" (under APPOINTMENTS)
   - The List Says: Availability settings
   - Our Gap: In the Appointments page, we have a button that says "Set Availability," but it leads to a generic
     settings page.
   - The Pro Fix: Doctors need a specialized Scheduler Tool where they can toggle their "On-Duty" status, set their
     consultation hours (e.g., 9 AM - 5 PM), and block out time for surgeries or breaks. This is crucial for the "Clinic
     Admin" to know when they can book patients for that doctor.

  2. The "Lens Recommendation" Module (under CONSULTATION WORKSPACE)
   - The List Says: Lens recommendation
   - Our Gap: In our dynamic workspace, we have a "Prescription (Drugs)" section, but we haven't built the specialized
     Optical Prescription Builder.
   - The Pro Fix: Eye doctors don't just write drug names; they need a grid to input Sphere (SPH), Cylinder (CYL), Axis,
     and Addition (ADD) for both eyes. We need a high-fidelity "Optical Rx" card that looks like a real optometrist's
     prescription pad.

  3. The "Uploaded Scans" Viewer (under PATIENT HISTORY)
   - The List Says: Uploaded scans
   - Our Gap: We have a "Recent History" sidebar, but it only shows text.
   - The Pro Fix: In an eye clinic, the Images are everything (Retina scans, OCTs). We need a "Clinical Imaging Gallery"
     inside the patient record/workspace where a doctor can actually click and expand thumbnails of eye scans.
